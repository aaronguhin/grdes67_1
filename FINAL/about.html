ABOUT INTNTS


INTNTS is a Los Angeles based skateboard company created in 2018 by Aaron Guhin. We make high quality skateboarding goods and we proudly make all our products in the USA. See, feel, and ride the pride with INTNTS.


DO YOU NEED TO SEND A RETURN FOR EXCHANGE OR REFUND?

If the items are unused we can accept returns for a exchange or a refund. To send a item back please mail it to:

Attention: Returns
INTNTS Skateboards
1234 Fake St.
Los Angeles, CA 90021

Just include a note and let us know what you would like in exchange and the proper sizing. If you would like a refund just include your order number. Thank you!
